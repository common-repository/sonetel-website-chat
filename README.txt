Sonetel's AI assisted website chat and call service for your business saves time, makes your business look professional and increases sales.

== Description ==

Increase sales by making your business look more professional and approachable. An easy-to-use solution for your customer
 service team.

* Customizable chat widget for your Wordpress site
* Free mobile apps for your team
* Artificial Intelligence suggesting answers that your team member can forward to the customer and save time.


https://player.vimeo.com/video/364036206

The chat widget is automatically shown in the language of the web site.

This plugin relies on [Sonetel's website chat service](https://sonetel.com/en/business-communication/).
See [Sonetel's privacy policy]( https://sonetel.com/en/help/help-topics/terms-conditions/privacy-policy/)

== Frequently Asked Questions ==

= Is there any cost for using the service ? =
The service is available for free to customers on our [paid plans](https://sonetel.com/plans).

= Are there any integrations ? =
Your customers can also ask questions with Facebook Messenger via your Facebook page. Other integrations are in the works.

= What languages are supported ? =
You can find a list [here](https://sonetel.com/en/business-communication/help/connect-website/languages-supported/)

= Do I need to configure it to make it work ? =
No. Default settings should work fine. The widget also detects the language of your site automatically. But you can naturally [change](https://sonetel.com/en/business-communication/help/connect-website/configure-the-business-messenger/) the look and feel and behaviour to your liking.
